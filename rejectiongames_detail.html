Rejection Games: Failure Coping Circle

Failure is a common experience. In this project, I gamify failure in the Engineering Science community by rewarding students with chocolates and candy when they share their rejections with the community.

Engineering Science is famous for being the most rigorous and demanding degree in Canada; the curriculum is designed so only half of the entering class will graduate. Such a high pressure program is often detrimental to the mental health of students. Rejection a commmon experience that often leads to a decrease in self esteem. By rewarding the bravery required to face rejection, we can build an open environment for sharing or failures and successes.

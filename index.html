Reducing Mental Illness Triggers

Engineering Science is a difficult program were almost half the students of the entering class do not finish the program. Challenging courses and demanding schedules throughout the 4 or 5 years in the program often triggers anxiety, depression and other mental illnesses in students. I am currently exploring different ways to build community in common spaces, provide better access to campus resources and partner with Engineering Administration to reduce risk factors for mental illness in the program.

To Discern Elegance -
A Design Journal

Investigating the design considerations that create delight and analyzing the mistakes that incubate misery is endlessly fascinating. Asking why things are a certain way becomes a different way of looking at the world. This method of observation, both an over analysis and a continuous appreciation, inspires the observer to also search for ways to improve the current experience. Thus a thought exercise gains the potential to reinvent and innovate.In this collection, I hope to share what catches my attention and propose ways to improve what frustrates me.
